Who we style

Laplex serves women aged 16–60 who refresh their look frequently.

Most of our customers replace braids or waves every 2–4 weeks. They trust us for dependable quality, quick installs at their local salon, and pricing that fits a regular styling routine.

  • Weekly and monthly stylers needing reliable restocks.
  • Salon clients looking for soft fiber that holds up between visits.
  • Retail shoppers.
